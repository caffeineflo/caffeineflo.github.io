Hi Visitor,

Welcome to my blog/website/notebook! I’m glad you are here and I hope you enjoy the content you find here. Here are a few words about me, so you’ll get to know who writes all this stuff.

Florian Harr

iOS Software Engineer in Atlanta

I’m an iOS Developer in Atlanta! I moved to Atlanta in 2014 right after I finished my Bachelors Degree in Computer Science in Stuttgart, Germany.

My move to the United States was inspired by a trip to New York in 2009 and a couple more trips to the US after that didn’t change my desire to move and life here. The 2009 trip was made possible by my great BroSin (Brother/Cousin) Heinrich Beck - Go checkout his blog.

After several visa attempts, getting my Bachelors degree and overcoming all bureaucracies I finally made it.

I currently work for Stable|Kernel on some interesting IoT projects in Atlanta, GA. I used to work for Big Nerd Ranch with some of the most brilliant people I have ever met!

I regularly speak at conferences, teach iOS coding or try to get involved with all kinds of community projects!

If you want to get in contact with me, either use this link or find me on LinkedIn. Resume, References and Portfolio are available upon request!
