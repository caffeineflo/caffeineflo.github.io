Two weeks ago I’ve attended the 360iDev conference in Denver. This year was my second year that I attended 360iDev. For those of you who don’t know what I’m talking about, it’s an iOS conference focusing on everything related to iOS Development. The conference lasts four days and usually has at least 3-4 different topics going at the same time so that everyone can find a topic they like at any given time.
