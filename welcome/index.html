Welcome!

Aug 30, 2016

You’ve made it! Welcome to my blog!

For years I wanted to write my own blog and share my opinion with the rest of the world. Now I finally took some time and created this little blog. I hope everyone can enjoy it!

Before you start reading through my blog, let me introduce myself: My name is Florian. I’m a German expat living in the US. Software Engineering is what I studied and what I currently do in my full time job. I’ll be writing about technical things like the coding I’m doing (iOS and Backend stuff), some of my hobbies (electric unicycling, 3D printing) or whatever comes into my mind.

I hope you enjoy it! Leave a comment if you do, otherwise constructive criticism is fine ;)
